# PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS

## Target Users
- Divorced/separated parents
- Their children

## Core Goal
- Reduce conflict
- Centralize communication
- Track family organization
- Maintain reliable records (especially medical)

## Features

### Profiles
Parents:
- first_name (required)
- last_name (required)
- email (login)
- phone
- address

Children:
- first_name
- last_name
- birthdate
- medical info
- school info

### Messaging
- Parent-only threads
- Family group chat

### Calendar
- custody management
- events
- medical appointments (with mandatory report)

### Medical Log
- chronological history per child

### Accounting
- shared expenses

### Gallery (SaaS only)
- photos + comments
